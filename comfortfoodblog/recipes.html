Hearty Vegetable Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 can white beans
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. Sauté carrots and onions.
  2. Add beans and tomatoes.
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes.
